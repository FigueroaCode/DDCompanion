5 modes of a DnD sessions
------
Setup
	Telling players about the adventure, details on quest, rolling checks for the rewards
Exploration
	Searching for traps, clues, and treasure
	Fills space between encounters
	Describe environment to allow them to choose an option ... Listen and decide if action requries a check
	Narrate results of their actions
Conversation
	Picking the brain of an NPC for information, Bluffs, diplomaacy, persuasion, insight, intimidation
Encounter and battle time
Passing Time
	A quick break, shouldn't spend a lot of time doing menial tasks like choosing tonight's dinner
----
Make note of passive skill modifiers
	If passive bonus is higher than the DC, it's a given
